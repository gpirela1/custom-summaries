Voice Fingerprint

Creating a voice fingerprint helps Chorus identify your voice more accurately, improving the quality of speaker separation and transcription for your calls. Chorus will attempt to create a voice fingerprint for you automatically after you record several calls, but we still recommend that you take a minute to register your voice fingerprint in order to get the best experience using Chorus.
