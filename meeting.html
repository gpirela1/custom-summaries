Executive Summary

This meeting served as an introductory QBR between ZoomInfo and Smartsheet, with a particular focus on introducing Chad Dyar, the new Director of Sales Enablement at Smartsheet, to the partnership. The session was intentionally kept high-level as a precursor to a more formal business review in the future. The ZoomInfo team presented an overview of how Smartsheet is currently utilizing their platform, reviewed the recently renewed contract, discussed current usage metrics, and shared upcoming product roadmap details with emphasis on AI-powered features and account intelligence capabilities.

The meeting revealed significant opportunities for Smartsheet to better leverage their ZoomInfo investment, as usage metrics showed lower-than-expected engagement. As Chad noted, "It's lower than I was expecting for the size of our sales organization," indicating room for improvement in adoption. The conversation concluded with Gabe's detailed roadmap presentation, which generated considerable interest from Chad regarding AI-powered account insights, potential integration with Smartsheet's upcoming account planning initiatives, and the newly announced Account AI API. The overall sentiment was positive and forward-looking, with both parties expressing interest in deeper strategic collaboration.

Customer/Prospect Context

Smartsheet recently renewed their contract with ZoomInfo for a 2-year term ending in April 2027. They are utilizing multiple ZoomInfo products including Co-Pilot (the AI-enhanced version of ZoomInfo), ZoomInfo Marketing, RingLead Cleanse for data hygiene, and Chorus for conversation intelligence (under a separate agreement expiring October 2026). The company has approximately 900 total licenses across these products.

Smartsheet is currently focused on enhancing their outbound prospecting efforts, with Chad mentioning, "We're looking to unlock some outbound prospecting efforts that we have coming up." They are also in the process of implementing a new coaching platform and planning to launch a "value selling" methodology in the coming quarter, replacing their current MEDIC qualification framework. Additionally, they're planning to implement an account planning tool, which presents integration opportunities with ZoomInfo's account intelligence capabilities.

The company is undergoing several strategic initiatives including territory design and segmentation under a newly hired director (Bill Mel), who will be managing a team of data stewards. As Thor mentioned, "He's responsible for territory design and segmentation... he's doing a look at the account data within ZoomInfo and evaluating it and determining his plan." This indicates Smartsheet is looking to enhance their data governance and GTM alignment strategies.

Gabe explained that when thinking about AI, they try to stay grounded on the jobs that sales teams need to do and how to make those more efficient.
